You can use the 「Backup and Restore」 feature to transfer data.
※ Data can only be transmitted between Android phones by now!
The operations are as follows:
  1. Back up data on your old phone
    「Setting」→「Backup and Restore」→ Select a cloud drive, click the button on right top to start backing up.
  2. Restore the that backup on your new phone
    「Setting」→「Backup and Restore」→ Select a cloud drive, after you sign in, select the backup in the list to restore the data.
